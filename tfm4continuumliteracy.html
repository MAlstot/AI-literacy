AI Literacy at Every Level

Same continuum, one more layer. On the previous page you saw how each level builds on the last and the cognitive payoff of placing an assignment there. Now open each level to add the piece that matters most for this program: the AI-literacy focus students can develop when an assignment is designed at that level.

The key idea: every level develops AI literacy — including Level 1, No AI Use. There is no "AI-literacy-free" assignment. At Level 1 the literacy comes from explaining to students why AI is set aside for this task, and weaving its focal points — AI’s limits, ethical boundaries, and integrity concerns — into your instruction and how you introduce the assignment. Naming where and why AI belongs is also your on-ramp to the AI disclosure statement you write in Module 6.

Literacy accumulates too — the focus at each level carries the ones below it forward.
