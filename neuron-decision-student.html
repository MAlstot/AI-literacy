Keep in mind. This yes-or-no is what one unit, called a perceptron, does deep inside the network, not what the whole model does. The model does not judge your prompt and answer yes or no. Millions of these tiny weightings run at once, and their combined result feeds a prediction, not a verdict. The clean inputs and whole-number weights here simply make the math visible; real units output values that slide between 0 and 1.
