Explore the Continuum

The IWU AI Literacy Continuum is cumulative: each level assumes the judgment of the levels beneath it and adds one more. Open each level to see three things — what it builds on, the cognitive payoff of placing an assignment there, and the AI literacy it develops. That last point is the heart of the framework: every level develops AI literacy — even Level 1, No AI Use, where the literacy comes from explaining to students why AI is set aside. "Higher" is not "better"; it is just more integrated.

Each level rests on all the ones before it — the filled blocks on each card show its foundation.

Supports MLO A4.1–A4.2 (the five levels, how they build, and the AI literacy each develops).
