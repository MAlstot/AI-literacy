How the Levels Build

The IWU AI Literacy Continuum is cumulative: each level assumes the judgment demanded by the levels beneath it and adds one more layer. "Higher" does not mean "better" — it means more AI integration, and more of the human oversight that makes it responsible. Open each level to see what it builds on and the cognitive payoff of placing an assignment there. Use the buttons to jump to a level.

Each level rests on all the ones before it — the filled blocks on each card show its foundation.

Supports MLO A4.1 (describe the five levels and how they build cumulatively) and A4.2 (how the 4Ds intensify across the continuum). Continuum adapted from the IWU 5-Level AI Literacy Continuum (Indiana Wesleyan University).
