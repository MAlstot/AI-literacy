Eleven Reasons: Where Might Your Assignment Invite Low Integrity?

Teach Forward · Module 6 · Adapted from Bertram Gallant & Rettinger (2025) and Megan Alstot’s Teaching for Integrity materials

Cheating has always existed. AI did not change why; it changed how.

Pick one assignment you know well, ideally the one you’re bringing to this module’s discussion. Research finds eleven reasons students cheat. Each comes with a question worth asking of your assignment. Check every reason that might be quietly present.

No blame here. Most good assignments invite two to four of these without anyone intending it; AI has made everything more challenging. Noticing is the mentoring move, and good teaching is still the best integrity builder.
